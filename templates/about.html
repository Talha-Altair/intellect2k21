About us

The Department of Computer Science & Engineering has been in existence since the inception of the Institution. Given the high demand for Computer Science professionals from industry, the department offers undergraduate programs,post-graduate degree programme and Doctoral Degree Program in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. All the programs offered are approved by the National Board of Accreditation, New Delhi.

Amidst of this pandemic to spread love, awareness, and knowledge to this world !. Intellect 2k20 is bought to you by the Department of Computer Science And Engineering of B.S Abdur Rahman Crescent Institute of Science and Technology. Intellect 2k20 is all about conducting Covid-19 Awareness Events with the slight brush of Technology over it as all of our offerings are informative and can be participated at your place Staying Home And Staying Safe!
